[**Open Source 101 Columbia**](http://opensource101.com/columbia/)  
Date: 2018-04-17  
Jason Hibbets and I will be presenting our talk *Avoiding the DevOps Pit of Misery: Tips and Tricks From the Trenches* together for the second time ever! [Open Source 101](http://opensource101.com/columbia/) is such a fantastic and inexpensive event (if you need assistance with a ticket let me know).

[**ChefConf 2018**](https://chefconf.chef.io/)
