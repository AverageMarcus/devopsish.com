On the podcast, the speaker asked the audience five questions. One of those questions was, "When was the last time you changed your mind?" This question struck me. When was the last time I changed my mind? What motivated me to make that change? Would I be able to help others make a similar change should they ask for assistance? My mind raced through a series of events where I felt my mind had changed on something.
